Chintan Shivir- 2-Day National Workshop

The Department of School Education and Literacy, Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD), is organising a 2 day National Workshop "ChintanShivir" to bring together key stakeholders from Government, NGOs and private sector to deliberate on 5 key areas of school education. The workshop will be organised on November 6-7 in New Delhi. Over the course of these 2 days, 150 experts will be divided into 5 groups to discuss recommendations and brainstorm on future course.
MHRD GOVT.OF INDIA
